How to reverse a withdrawal

In order to cancel a withdrawal, login to your account in Station HorizonLite™ and click on Account > Transaction Activity, A screen will open automatically, showing all your withdrawal requests that are still pending, Select the withdrawal requests to be reversed and click on Cancel Withdraw button. The withdrawal request/s will be cancelled and the withdrawal funds will be immediately returned to your Station HorizonLite™ account. The option of cancelling a withdrawal is especially useful when you do not have sufficient funds in your account but you still wish to continue activities. Cancelling the withdrawal process will make these funds immediately available in your account
